What is StudentsxCEOs Summit?

StudentsxCEOs Summit is a student-run business event that gathers top business leaders in Indonesia organized by to share knowledge, insight, and experience to selected students across nation. This event is also a platform for students to build networks and earn skill through various workshops provided. Through this event, we intend to create business event that shapes of Indonesia's top selected students to be the future business leaders through knowledge exchange, capacity building, and networking opportunity.
